Project info

Date

Feb 15, 2024

Category

Garden

Space Plan

2 Bedrooms, Office

Timeline

2 Weeks

This urban apartment offers peaceful escapes from the noise of the city. Clean lines, muted colors, and thoughtful design choices create a calming and functional environment.

The process

1. Urban Reset Vision

We focused on creating a visual exhale—soft tones, decluttered spaces, and smart zoning brought a soothing atmosphere.

2. Intelligent Layouts

We reimagined the small footprint by optimizing flow and defining zones with light, texture, and built-ins.

3. Subtle Luxury

Understated elegance came through in matte finishes, layered lighting, and premium textiles that quietly elevate the experience.
